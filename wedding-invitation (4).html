Finish & Send Invitations

Guest links are short because only the guest's own name and photo travel through the link — the names, venues, times, and QR codes live inside the page itself, not in every link.

Step 1 — Lock in the shared details

Edit the bride/groom names, venues, times, message, and QR codes above until they look right.

This downloads a finished .html file with all of the above baked in. Host that downloaded file (Netlify, GitHub Pages, your own site, etc.) — that hosted copy is what you'll actually link guests to.
Step 2 — Generate each guest's short link

On the hosted page: set the guest circle above to one guest's name and photo, then click Generate. Copy the link, send it, then change the circle to the next guest and generate again.

Skipping the guest photo (name only) makes the link even shorter.
